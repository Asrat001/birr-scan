# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [2026/1/9]

This Privacy Policy describes how **[Birr Sacn]** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Overview

**[Birr Sacn]** is a utility application designed to help restaurant servers track and audit their earnings per shift by capturing payment screenshots or photos and extracting transaction amounts.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. We **do not sell, rent, or share your data** with third parties.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Images and Screenshots

* The app allows users to **capture or select screenshots/photos** of payment receipts.
* These images are used **only** to extract transaction amounts.
* Images are **stored locally on your device** unless you choose to delete them.

### b) Extracted Transaction Data

* Extracted information may include:

  * Transaction amount
  * Date and time (if available)
  * Payment method (if detected)

This data is used to help users **audit their earnings per shift**.

### c) Personal Information

We **do not require account registration** and **do not collect**:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Location data
* Payment card details

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

* Extract payment amounts from screenshots or photos
* Display daily or shift-based earnings summaries
* Help users audit and manage their income

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

* All data is stored **locally on your device**.
* We do **not upload or transmit** images or transaction data to external servers.
* We use Android’s built-in security features to protect stored data.

---

## 5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

* **Camera** – to capture payment screenshots or photos
* **Storage / Media Access** – to select existing images from your device

These permissions are used **only for the app’s core functionality**.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not share**, sell, or disclose your data to:

* Advertisers
* Analytics providers
* Third parties

All data remains under your control on your device.

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

* All stored images and transaction records can be deleted by the user at any time within the app.
* Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

**[Birr Scan]** is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the **Last updated** date above.
